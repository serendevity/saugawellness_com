OUR VISION

Our vision is to increase awareness and accessibilty to existing healthcare resources by proactively engaging marginalized and racialized communities and new immigrants in their own languages.

What We Offer

Health Coaching

We help you navigate the medical, emotional, psychological, visual and dental resources and support systems. We offer translation services and assistance in your medical appointments.

Senior Support

We offer active lifestyle and wellness classes & programs to our seniors. We have seminars and webinars with open forum Q & A on your health concerns. We create volunteer opportunities for seniors to stay socially active and connected.

MBCT

We offer mindfulness-based cognitive therapy. It incorporates meditation, breathing exercises, and other elements of mindfulness along with cognitive behavior therapy. This approach is proven to have positive impact on physical and mental health.

Mission

To Ensure you are happy, healthy and heard.

We are dedicated to building healthy and resilient communities. Our mission is to make the health & wellness industry more accessible and approachable for people who cannot afford expensive gym memberships and valuable advice from health and wellness industry experts. Sauga Wellness Foundation will improve the accessibility of underserved communities that face language barriers and socioeconomic disadvantages, to alternate health care therapies and resources with focus on prevention rather than cure.

An ounce of prevention is better then a pound of cure. About half of all adults live with one or more chronic conditions. Many of these chronic conditions are preventable by learning to manage our lifestyles.

Services We Offer

Individual Services

A dedicated mentor will personalized one-on-one coaching by finding the food and lifestyle that works best for you. We'll provide you wit health care resources that will help you navigate the wellness path that works best for you. Our emphasis is on self love and self care. If you take care of yourself, you will be in a much better position to take care of your loved ones.

Family Services

Work as a team. Get your family involved into a better lifestyle that keep you and your loving circle happy and healthy. Instead of conflicts and arguments, strive for laughter, compassion and understanding. We offer all kinds of supports and programs that empower you to take charge of your physical, emontional and mental health.

New Comers Services

We offer settlements services to all students, immigrants, refugees and naturalized Canadians. We offer information, resources and guidance in all languages, including, Dari, Pashto, Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, Punjabi & Bengali. Our programs are inclusive & welcome people from all faiths, gender,ethnicity & sexual orientation.

About Us

Sauga Wellness Foundation is a not for profit organization. We are committed to building resilient and healthy communities. We are a group of health and wellness advocates and activists dedicated to improving the lives of the people we serve. Our mission is to share our knowledge and experiences with our community to improve your health and wellness, & overall quality of life.
